About

Tia Katariina

Hi, my name is Tia Katariina. I'm a beauty enthusiast. I love to learn, write, try new things and share my experiences when it comes to beauty, skincare and wellness. Let me do the dirty work and research to save you from the trouble!

I was born and raised in Finland. I grew up being a shy kid who always believed to be the "ugly one" and really struggled with confidence when it came to the way I looked. Later, as a teenager, I started trying all types of products and methods and ways to change that. I wanted to look good and feel good. Many things I had to learn the hard way, which is why I have now a lot of experience that I would love to share.

Now I am 25 years old. I wanted to move abroad, I love Finland but only in the summer. I can't stand the cold. So 2022 I moved to Tenerife for a year, and since July 2023 I've lived in Lisbon, Portugal. I went through quite a few episodes because of my experiments. We went from severe acne and my hair falling from bleaching to a clean skin and my hair growing back strong.

I learned my lessons and that is why today I research my things carefully and really believe and listen people and their warnings.
